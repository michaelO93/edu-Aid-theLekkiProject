What is Edu-Aid

Edu-Aid is a Non-Government Organization of Covenant Christian Centre established in 2005 with the intent of facilitating access to qualitative public primary education in Nigeria.

assignment

What We Do

We support selected public primary schools that are within the vicinity of the three centres of Covenant Christian Centre through innovative programs and activities geared towards improving the quality of public primary education

my_location

Where We Are

We present support seven(7) schools on the Mainland Area, five(5) of which are located within the Yaba Church axis, while the other(2) are within the Iganmu Church axis.

THE LEKKI
PROJECT

Edu-Aid is now in Lekki. We have approval to support five(5) schools within the Lekki Area. All the schools are located around the Covenant Chapel, Lekki. The five schools are as follows:

1 Maiyegun Community Primary School, Jakande

2 Ilasan Primary School, Jakande

3 Ikota Primary School, Ikota

4 Ajiran Community Primary School, Agungi Ajiran

5 Lagos State Model Primary School, Marwa
